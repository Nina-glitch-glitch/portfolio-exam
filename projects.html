Overview

This portfolio was created as part of my UX and web development studies, with the goal of presenting my work in a clear, structured, and honest way. Rather than focusing solely on polished visuals, I wanted to highlight my process — how I think, why I make certain decisions, and what I learn along the way.

Problem

A portfolio is a first impression, and viewers decide within seconds whether to stay or leave. Research showed that clarity, simple navigation and fast understanding are crucial for engagement. My challenge was to design a portfolio that communicates who I am and how I work — clearly, quickly and without visual overload. It needed to feel professional and structured, yet still personal enough to build trust and make people want to explore further and eventually reach out.

Solution

I conducted qualitative interviews and a survey to understand what viewers expect from a portfolio. The findings shaped the structure, content and priorities of the design. Clear navigation, consistent storytelling and fast comprehension became the core design requirements. “Keep it simple” became the core design principle for this portfolio. When clarity increases, engagement follows.
